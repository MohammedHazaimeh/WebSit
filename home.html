The introduction


The agricultural sector is an important and strategic sector that provides added value to a number of sectors such as transport, labor, energy, industry and trade. The growth in the sector is a distinct case, especially if it is identified alongside the partner sectors and with a contribution of up to 20% to the national income and a growth rate of up to 4%, Where we look forward to developing the role of agricultural extension to be the advanced arm in simulating farmers and giving them benefit and service by all direct and indirect means. And scientific knowledge of the place and nature of agriculture, in addition to the direct field role in communicating with farmers in their places.

Founding date

The Ministry of Agriculture was established during the era of the Emirate of Transjordan in the formation of the thirteenth ministry on 1929

Ministry's vision

Efficient agricultural services that meet the needs of citizens with high quality and effectiveness, and the advancement of the institutional and individual capabilities of the Ministry and the organization of the agricultural sector in line with national goals

Institutional Objectives of the Ministry of Agriculture

1- An efficient and effective ministry. 2- A globally competitive agricultural private sector. 3- A secure and stable agricultural society. 4- Affiliate and participating farmers.
