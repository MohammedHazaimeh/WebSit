General information for the Ministry of Agriculture

The core values ​​of the ministry:


1- Agriculture is a national priority.
2- Respecting the legislation.
3- Integrity and transparency.
4- Initiative and improvement.
5- Participation.
6- Efficiency and effectiveness of performance.

National Objectives:


1- An efficient and effective government.
2- A globally competitive private sector.
3- A secure and stable society.
4- Affiliated and participating citizens.

Institutional Objectives of the Ministry of Agriculture:


1- An efficient and effective ministry.
2- A globally competitive agricultural
private sector.
3- A secure and stable agricultural society.
4- Affiliate and participating farmers.

For institutions whose board of directors is chaired by His Excellency the Minister of Agriculture:


1- An efficient and effective ministry.
2- A globally competitive agricultural
private sector.
3- A secure and stable agricultural society.
4- Affiliate and participating farmers.
